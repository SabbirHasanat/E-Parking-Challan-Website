Parking Challan Information

This page provides detailed information about parking challans.

What is a Parking Challan?

A parking challan is a ticket issued by traffic authorities when a vehicle is parked in violation of parking rules and regulations.

Common Violations

  • Parking in a no-parking zone
  • Parking in a disabled parking spot without a valid permit
  • Parking in a fire lane
  • Exceeding the allowed parking time

How to Pay a Parking Challan

You can pay a parking challan by visiting the payment page or using the mobile app.
